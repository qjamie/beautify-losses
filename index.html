Multi-attack Mode - How to use

With this selected, losses can be separated based on the attacking/defending player.

This requires minor formatting changes - here, you'll have to separate each batch of losses with the date - see below as an example:

Date: November 18th, 2023
20:05:17 DEAD UNIT 52 x Marauder died Attacking City: Owned by javitxu attacked City #7255448 with intent Attack (Fortress) at 285, 450
20:04:48 DEAD UNIT 68 x Forsaken died Attacking City: Owned by javitxu attacked City #7255448 with intent Attack (Fortress) at 285, 450

Date: November 18th, 2023
19:55:15 DEAD UNIT 1 x Marauder died Attacking City: Owned by 6283808 attacked City #7255374 with intent Attack (Fortress) at 305, 518
19:55:03 DEAD UNIT 2 x Chaos Warhounds died Attacking City: Owned by 6283808 attacked City #7255374 with intent Attack (Fortress) at 305, 518

Date: November 18th, 2023
15:12:32 DEAD UNIT 52 x Chaos Spawn of Khorne died Attacking City: Owned by Lordette34 attacked City #7261790 with intent Attack (Fortress) at 410, 169
15:11:11 DEAD UNIT 798 x Marauder died Attacking City: Owned by Lordette34 attacked City #7261790 with intent Attack (Fortress) at 410, 169
15:10:28 DEAD UNIT 5,233 x Chaos Spawn of Khorne died Attacking City: Owned by Lordette34 attacked City #7261790 with intent Attack (Fortress) at 410, 169

Hospital unitsmustalso be formatted the same way, as the dates are used as 'separators'. You can use the times to line each attack up manually. The application will break and give wrong results if this is not applied.
